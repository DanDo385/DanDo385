I spent 13 years across institutional fixed income sales, trading, and portfolio management, including Merrill Lynch, Jefferies, Nomura, PointState, and Prudential / PGIM.

That background gives me a practical view of how capital actually moves: counterparty relationships, risk plumbing, settlement frictions, liquidity, execution, portfolio construction, and market structure.

Now I build and study the systems that may replace or upgrade that wiring: AI workflows, Ethereum infrastructure, DeFi protocols, stablecoins, RWA infrastructure, and agent-readable web surfaces.
